Why Choose FluxGlove?

Cutting-Edge Innovation

FluxGlove is a cutting-edge wearable device that brings human hand movements into the digital world with unmatched accuracy. Equipped with multiple flex sensors and a high-performance IMU, it captures every gesture and subtle motion with minimal latency. Our mission is to make hand motion a first-class input for both professionals and enthusiasts, bridging the gap between the physical and digital realms. Designed for comfort, versatility, and reliability, FluxGlove is ideal for VR applications, robotics, and creative software.

Simple & Intuitive

Getting Started

Getting started with FluxGlove is simple and intuitive. First, connect your glove to your computer via WebSerial or Bluetooth. Once connected, calibrate the sensors to ensure maximum accuracy and responsiveness. You can then map gestures to actions on your software, games, or robotics platform. The interface allows you to test your gestures in real time and save personalized profiles for seamless interaction across applications. FluxGlove is designed to adapt to your workflow, offering a fully customizable experience tailored to your hands.
